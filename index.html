Frequently Asked Questions

What are your business hours?

We operate from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. However, all bookings must be completed by 1:00 PM.

When should I make payment for my order?

Full payment is required before we begin production. Payment is non-refundable, except in rare situations (a 10% admin fee will apply).

How can I avoid payment issues?

Always confirm account details before making any transfers. We are not responsible for payments sent to the wrong account.

When is my order processed?

Orders are only processed after full payment is received and confirmed. Due to Nigerian banking system delays, wait time starts after confirmation, not after transfer.

Can I make changes to my order after placing it?

Yes, but you must request any changes (flavor, size, or timing) within 15 minutes of placing the order.

Do you charge extra for urgent orders?

Yes, extra charges apply for orders placed on short notice.

What are the pick-up guidelines?

Pick-ups must be done on or before 4:45 PM, as our premises close by 5:00 PM. Uncollected items will be refrigerated until the next day.

Will you arrange emergency deliveries if I change my mind?

No. If a customer changes their mind about delivery after an agreement, they must handle the logistics themselves.

When do deliveries happen?

Deliveries run from 10:00 AM to 4:30 PM, or later by prior agreement.

Do you offer dedicated riders for delivery?

No, we do not assign individual riders per customer. Our dispatch system allocates deliveries.

What happens if the rider canâ€™t reach me during delivery?

Riders will call before delivery. If they can't reach you, your items will return to the factory. You can then either pick them up or reschedule delivery.

Do you deliver everywhere in Abuja?

No. We do not deliver to certain areas such as Gwagwalada, Nyanya, Kuje, and Suleja. Deliveries may also be delayed during rain for rider safety.
